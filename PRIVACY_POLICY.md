# Privacy Policy — ManaHub

**Last updated: April 12, 2026**

This Privacy Policy describes how ManaHub ("the App", "we", "us") handles information when you use the ManaHub Android application. Please read it carefully.

**Short version:** ManaHub does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to its developers or any third-party analytics service. Your card collection, game history, and preferences are stored only on your device.

## 2. Information We Do NOT Collect

ManaHub does **not** collect, transmit to its servers, or sell:

- Your name, email address, or any account information (no account is required)
- Location data
- Contacts or social graph data
- Browsing history outside the App
- Any analytics or crash data through third-party SDKs (no Firebase Analytics, no Crashlytics, no Sentry)

**Note on the Send Feedback feature:** If you choose to use the optional feedback feature (Section 4.3), your message text and any image you attach are sent directly to the developer's email address via your device's email application. This is the only scenario in which any content you create in ManaHub can leave your device — and only when you explicitly initiate it.

---

## 3. Information Stored Locally on Your Device

All data generated by your use of the App is stored **exclusively on your device** using Android's Room database and DataStore Preferences. This data is never transmitted to ManaHub's servers.

Data stored locally includes:

| Data | Purpose | Storage |
|------|---------|---------|
| Card collection entries (card names, quantities, conditions, languages, foil flags) | Collection management | Room database |
| Deck lists and configurations | Deck builder | Room database |
| Game session history (life totals, turn count, duration, winner, player names) | Statistics and profile | Room database |
| Post-game survey answers | Analytics and deck suggestions | Room database |
| Tournament data (players, matches, standings) | Tournament organiser | Room database |
| Card metadata cache (oracle text, images URLs, prices from Scryfall) | Display and tagging | Room database |
| Player name, avatar URL, preferred currency, app language, card language | Personalisation | DataStore Preferences |
| Tag dictionary overrides and user-defined tags | Custom tagging | DataStore Preferences |
| App theme, auto-refresh toggle, tag thresholds | Settings | DataStore Preferences |

**You can delete all locally stored data at any time by uninstalling the App.** Android's standard app data clearing mechanism (Settings → Apps → ManaHub → Clear Data) also removes all stored data.

## 4. Third-Party Services

ManaHub connects to external services to provide certain features. These services have their own privacy policies, which we encourage you to review.

### 4.1 Scryfall API

**What it does:** Provides card data including names, oracle text, images, prices, and set information.

**What is sent:** Card names or Scryfall card IDs as search query parameters in HTTPS requests. No personal information is included in these requests.

**Data received:** Card metadata, prices, and image URLs. This data is cached locally in the Room database.

**Scryfall's privacy policy:** [https://scryfall.com/docs/privacy](https://scryfall.com/docs/privacy)

ManaHub complies with Scryfall's API usage guidelines, including the required `User-Agent` header and a request rate limit of ≤10 requests per second.

### 4.2 YouTube Data API v3 (Draft Guide feature)

**What it does:** Fetches Magic: The Gathering draft guide videos for a given set.

**What is sent:** MTG set names as search query parameters in HTTPS requests to the YouTube Data API. No personal information is included.

**Data received:** Video titles, thumbnails, descriptions, and YouTube video IDs. This data is displayed in-app and is not stored permanently.

**Google's privacy policy:** [https://policies.google.com/privacy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

**YouTube Terms of Service:** [https://www.youtube.com/t/terms](https://www.youtube.com/t/terms)

Clicking a video opens it in the YouTube app or browser; ManaHub does not embed YouTube players.

### 4.3 Send Feedback Feature

**What it does:** Lets you compose a message and optionally attach an image from your device's photo library, then send it to the developer.

**How it works — no data is collected by ManaHub:**
- Tapping "Send Feedback" opens a bottom sheet where you type your message and optionally select an image.
- When you tap "Send", your device's email app opens with the message and attachment pre-filled. You choose your email app and explicitly press Send inside that app.
- ManaHub never transmits your message or image to any server. The transmission is handled entirely by the email application you select.

**What data is involved:**
- Your message text (plain text only).
- An image you explicitly choose from your photo library (optional). The image is passed as a scoped `content://` URI directly to your email client; ManaHub does not copy, store, or read the image file.

**EXIF metadata notice:** Photos taken with a camera may contain embedded EXIF metadata, including GPS coordinates, device model, and timestamps. If you attach an image, this metadata is included in the file sent by your email app. By choosing to attach an image, you accept that this metadata may accompany it. If you are concerned about metadata, remove EXIF data from the image before attaching it (many gallery apps offer this option).

**No automatic sending:** Feedback is never sent without your explicit action. You must tap "Send" inside your chosen email app for anything to be transmitted.

**Email app privacy:** The privacy practices of the email application you choose are governed by that application's own privacy policy. ManaHub has no control over how the email app handles your data after the Intent is fired.

### 4.4 RSS and Atom News Feeds

**What it does:** Fetches publicly available Magic: The Gathering news articles from community sources (MTG Goldfish, Channel Fireball, and others as configured).

**What is sent:** Standard HTTPS GET requests to the feed URLs. No personal information is transmitted.

**Data received:** Article titles, descriptions, URLs, and publication dates. Content is displayed in-app and cached temporarily.

**Note:** You can view, enable, or disable individual news sources within the App. ManaHub does not control the content or privacy practices of third-party news sources.

## 5. Camera Permission

ManaHub requests the `CAMERA` permission for the Card Scanner feature.

- The camera is used **solely to capture card names via on-device OCR (ML Kit Text Recognition)**.
- **No camera frames, images, or OCR results are transmitted to any external server.** All text recognition is performed entirely on your device.
- The camera is accessed only when you navigate to the Scanner screen.
- You can deny the camera permission and still use all other features of the App.
- If you deny the permission permanently, you can re-enable it at any time in your device's Settings → Apps → ManaHub → Permissions.

---

## 6. Internet Permission

ManaHub requests the `INTERNET` permission to communicate with the external APIs described in Section 4 (Scryfall, YouTube Data API, and RSS feeds).

All network traffic initiated by ManaHub uses HTTPS. Cleartext (HTTP) connections are blocked at the OS level via Android's Network Security Configuration.

The Send Feedback feature (Section 4.3) does **not** use ManaHub's INTERNET permission. It delegates to your device's email app via an Android Intent; all network communication for email transmission is performed by that app under its own permissions.

---

## 7. Backup and Cloud Storage

By default, Android can back up app data to Google Drive. ManaHub explicitly **excludes** its Room database, DataStore preferences, and app-specific SharedPreferences files from Google Drive backup using Android's data extraction rules (`dataExtractionRules` and `fullBackupContent`).

This means:
- Your card collection and game history are **not** backed up to Google Drive.
- If you change devices or reinstall the App, your data will not be restored automatically.
- This is intentional to prevent your private collection data from being copied to other devices or cloud environments without your knowledge.

## 9. Data Security

Because ManaHub does not transmit personal data to external servers:

- There is no server-side data breach risk.
- Your card collection, game history, and preferences are protected by your device's standard Android security model (app sandboxing, file system permissions).
- Release builds of ManaHub use R8 code minification and obfuscation, reducing the surface area for reverse-engineering attacks.

We recommend using a screen lock on your device and keeping Android up to date to protect locally stored data.
