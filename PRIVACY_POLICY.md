# Privacy Policy — ManaHub

**Last updated: April 17, 2026**

This Privacy Policy describes how ManaHub ("the App", "we", "us") collects, uses, and protects information when you use the ManaHub Android application. Please read it carefully.

**Short version:** ManaHub works fully without an account — your local collection, decks, and game history stay on your device. If you choose to create an account, we collect your email address, a nickname you choose, and a unique game identifier assigned by us. This data is stored securely on Supabase servers and is used solely to provide account features. We do not sell your data or use it for advertising.

---

## 2. Account Registration (Optional)

Creating an account is entirely optional. All core features of the App (card collection, deck builder, game tracker, statistics) are available without an account and without providing any personal data.

If you choose to create an account, you may do so via:

- **Email and password** — you provide your email address, a password, and a nickname.
- **Google Sign-In** — you authorise Google to share your email address and display name with the App.

By creating an account you acknowledge that you are at least 13 years of age (or the minimum digital consent age in your jurisdiction).

---

## 3. Information We Collect

### 3.1 When you create an account

| Data | Source | Purpose |
|------|--------|---------|
| Email address | You (or Google) | Account authentication and identification |
| Password | You | Authentication — **stored as a bcrypt hash by Supabase; never readable by the developer** |
| Nickname | You | Display name shown in the App (not unique; you choose it freely) |
| Game Tag (e.g. `#A3KX9Z`) | Auto-generated by the server | Unique identifier for your account; used for future player-search features |
| Avatar URL | You (optional, via the avatar picker) | Profile personalisation |
| Authentication provider (`email` or `google`) | System | Identifying your sign-in method |

### 3.2 Data stored locally (no account required)

All of the following is stored **only on your device** and is never transmitted to our servers unless explicitly noted:

| Data | Purpose | Storage |
|------|---------|---------|
| Card collection (card names, quantities, conditions, languages, foil flags) | Collection management | Room database (local) |
| Deck lists and configurations | Deck builder | Room database (local) |
| Game session history (life totals, duration, winner, player names) | Statistics and profile | Room database (local) |
| Post-game survey answers | Analytics and deck suggestions | Room database (local) |
| Tournament data (players, matches, standings) | Tournament organiser | Room database (local) |
| Card metadata cache (oracle text, image URLs, prices from Scryfall) | Display and tagging | Room database (local) |
| Player name, avatar URL, preferred currency, app language, card language | Personalisation | DataStore Preferences (local) |
| Tag dictionary overrides and user-defined tags | Custom tagging | DataStore Preferences (local) |
| App theme, auto-refresh toggle, tag thresholds | Settings | DataStore Preferences (local) |

### 3.3 Data we do NOT collect

- Location data
- Contacts or social graph data
- Browsing history outside the App
- Analytics or behavioural tracking data
- Crash reports (no Crashlytics, Firebase Analytics, or Sentry are integrated)

---

## 4. How We Use Your Data

Data collected through account registration is used exclusively to:

- Authenticate you when you sign in.
- Display your nickname and game tag within the App.
- Associate your profile avatar with your account.
- Enable future features that require user identification (e.g., player search, friend lists).

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose other than operating the App's account features.

---

## 5. Data Retention and Deletion

Your account data is retained for as long as your account exists.

**You can permanently delete your account and all associated data at any time** from the App: Profile screen → account icon → "Delete Account". Deletion is immediate and irreversible. It removes your record from Supabase's authentication system and the `user_profiles` table.

Local data (card collection, game history, settings) is stored only on your device. It is deleted when you uninstall the App or use Settings → Apps → ManaHub → Clear Data.

---

## 6. Third-Party Services

ManaHub connects to the following external services. Each has its own privacy policy.

### 6.1 Supabase (account data processor)

**What it does:** Provides authentication (email/password sign-up, sign-in, password reset, Google OAuth) and stores your account profile (`nickname`, `game_tag`, `avatar_url`).

**Data stored:** Email address, hashed password, nickname, game tag, avatar URL, authentication provider.

**Server location:** EU West 2 (London, United Kingdom).

**Supabase Privacy Policy:** [https://supabase.com/privacy](https://supabase.com/privacy)

Supabase acts as a **data processor** on our behalf. We do not grant Supabase permission to use your data for any purpose other than providing authentication and storage services to the App.

### 6.2 Google Sign-In

**What it does:** Allows you to create an account or sign in using your existing Google account.

**Data shared with ManaHub:** Your email address and Google display name (used as initial nickname). No other Google account data is accessed.

**Google's Privacy Policy:** [https://policies.google.com/privacy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

Using Google Sign-In is optional. You may instead create an account with an email and password.

### 6.3 Scryfall API

**What it does:** Provides card data including names, oracle text, images, prices, and set information.

**What is sent:** Card names or Scryfall card IDs as search query parameters in HTTPS requests. No personal information is included.

**Scryfall Privacy Policy:** [https://scryfall.com/docs/privacy](https://scryfall.com/docs/privacy)

### 6.4 YouTube Data API v3 (Draft Guide feature)

**What it does:** Fetches Magic: The Gathering draft guide videos for a given set.

**What is sent:** MTG set names as search query parameters. No personal information is transmitted.

**Google's Privacy Policy:** [https://policies.google.com/privacy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

### 6.5 Send Feedback Feature

Tapping "Send Feedback" opens your device's email app with a pre-filled message addressed to the developer. ManaHub does not transmit your message to any server — the email app handles transmission entirely. See the original note in this section regarding EXIF metadata in attached images.

### 6.6 RSS and Atom News Feeds

Standard HTTPS GET requests to publicly available MTG news feed URLs. No personal information is transmitted.

## 7. Nickname Moderation

To maintain a respectful community, the App enforces a server-side content policy on nicknames. Nicknames are checked against a list of prohibited terms before being saved. If your nickname contains inappropriate content, it will be rejected and you will be asked to choose another.

This check is performed entirely on our servers. The list of prohibited terms is never sent to your device.

---

## 8. Camera Permission

ManaHub requests the `CAMERA` permission for the Card Scanner feature. The camera is used solely for on-device OCR (ML Kit Text Recognition) to identify card names. No camera frames or images are transmitted to any server.

---

## 9. Internet Permission

ManaHub requests the `INTERNET` permission to communicate with the services described in Section 6. All network traffic uses HTTPS. Cleartext (HTTP) connections are blocked at the OS level via Android's Network Security Configuration.

---

## 10. Backup and Cloud Storage

ManaHub explicitly **excludes** its local Room database, DataStore preferences, and Supabase session tokens from Android's Google Drive backup mechanism. This means:

- Your local card collection and game history are **not** backed up to Google Drive.
- Your Supabase authentication session is **not** transferred to other devices via backup.
- Account data (nickname, game tag, avatar) is stored on Supabase servers and is accessible on any device where you sign in to your account.

---

## 11. Data Security

- **In transit:** All communication between the App and Supabase uses HTTPS/TLS.
- **Passwords:** Stored as bcrypt hashes by Supabase. The developer has no access to your password.
- **Session tokens:** Stored in Android DataStore, excluded from system backups, and protected by Android's app sandboxing.
- **At rest:** Data on Supabase servers is protected by Supabase's infrastructure security. See [https://supabase.com/security](https://supabase.com/security).
- **App binary:** Release builds use R8 code minification, reducing the surface area for reverse-engineering attacks.
- **Row-Level Security:** Each user can only read and modify their own profile row in the database. No user can access another user's account data.
